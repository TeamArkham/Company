About

BlurryFace is a non -listed company formed by the 2 co-founders Tyler and Josh . The main work of the company is to produce music for the Rock-Band 21 pilots. Formed in 2010, it helped carry the brand name "TWENTY ONE PILOTS" to main stream recognition, and helped produce the first debut album for the band.

On successful indie albums, Blurryface was recognised by Atlantic Records and was signed and 21 pilots debuted with their first smash album Vessel

The duo achieved breakthrough success with their fourth album, Blurryface (2015), which produced the successful singles "Stressed Out" and "Ride"
